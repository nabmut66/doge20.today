Frequently Asked Questions

Dogecoin20 is part of the Dogecoin family of meme coins but adds more utility and value for holders by introducing staking on Ethereum for passive rewards. The '20' in Dogecoin20 stands for 'ERC-20'.

DOGE20 is an ERC-20 token built on the Ethereum blockchain, which has smart contract capabilities. The DOGE20 staking contract will issue rewards to each staker over the next two years, according to their percentage of the staking pool.

The DOGE20 token is greener and more versatile than the original Dogecoin because it runs on Ethereum’s Proof-of-Stake network, which is easily accessible and does not require a Proof-of-Work mining setup. As an ERC-20 token it is also easy to store in any popular self-custody wallet.

DOGE20 has a fixed total supply of 140,000,000,000 tokens with 35,000,000,000 sold in the presale. By not having an infinite supply like Dogecoin, we prevent constant inflation and create scarcity, causing positive price pressure.
